# B2C Marketing 101

A comprehensive guide and execution skill for direct-to-consumer marketing campaigns.

## What This Skill Does

Helps B2C brands build and execute effective marketing campaigns across:

- **Brand Positioning**: Defining unique value proposition and messaging
- **Customer Acquisition**: Paid social, search, and organic strategies
- **Email Marketing**: Welcome sequences, promotional campaigns, retention
- **Social Media**: Content strategy for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook
- **Conversion Optimization**: Landing pages, checkout flow, offers
- **Analytics**: Tracking CAC, LTV, ROAS, and key growth metrics

## Who This Is For

Founders, marketers, and growth teams at D2C brands looking to:
- Launch their first marketing campaigns
- Improve existing channel performance
- Build scalable acquisition systems
- Optimize customer retention and LTV
