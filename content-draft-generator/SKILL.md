# Content Draft Generator

Rapidly generates high-quality first drafts for any type of content.

## What This Skill Does

- Creates complete content drafts from minimal input (topic, outline, or brief)
- Matches specified tone of voice and brand guidelines
- Integrates target keywords naturally for SEO
- Structures content with proper headings and flow
- Includes compelling introductions and CTAs

## How to Use

Provide:
1. The topic or content brief
2. Target audience and tone
3. Target keywords (optional)
4. Desired length and format

The skill will produce a complete draft ready for review and editing.
